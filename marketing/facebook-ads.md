# Stayful Intelligence — Facebook Ad Copy

Goal: convert cold/warm traffic into **free-trial subscribers** (account = email + mobile, 5 free reports, no card).
Landing page: `intelligence.stayful.co.uk` → postcode box → `/signup`.

**Voice rules (from the brand):** Direct, honest, number-led. Never guarantee income — say "comparable properties achieve" / "typically see." Lead with **5 free reports**, never "14-day trial" (the FAQ copy is wrong; code gates on 5 runs).

**Meta policy note:** Property/income ads get flagged for implied financial guarantees. Keep claims sourced and conditional ("based on live comparables near your postcode"), never "you will earn £X." The honest voice helps you here — don't undo it in the ad.

## Creative direction (for whoever builds the assets)

- **Format:** Single image or 15–30s video. The site already has a 2-min walkthrough of a real York run — cut a 15s teaser of the report generating + the net-profit and quiet-month sections.
- **Static concept 1:** Phone/laptop mock of the report's Revenue Breakdown, big net figure, small "near YO1 · live comps" caption. Overlay: "What could this earn? ~20s. Free."
- **Static concept 2:** Side-by-side "Estimated £30,940 → Actual £34,727" with "the model vs the result" — strong, honest, scroll-stopping.
- **Static concept 3:** Two columns "Long-let net vs Short-let net" with a month strip showing the dip — sells the honesty angle.
- Keep text on image under ~20% area. Brand: clean, number-led, no hype graphics.
